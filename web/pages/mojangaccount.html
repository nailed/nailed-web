Link your mojang/minecraft account

You can link your mojang or minecraft account to your Nailed account, so we know for sure it's you who is playing. Because of that, you do not longer need to log in ingame.

We do not store your password in our database. We only send it to the mojang servers and ask if the account is valid. After we asked that, we remove your username and password from our database and we only store if your account is valid or not.
